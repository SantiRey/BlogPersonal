papers

Natural language classifier based on Reddit posts for information retrieval

This work aims at trying to interpret input from user by deriving benefit from textual clues embedded in the input by using a collected dataset from many thousands of reddit posts in three different subreddits: (politics, datascience, listentothis). We also discuss the implication concluded from the picked classifier for training the datasets and how it was optimal for this work in comparison with other classifiers.

Desing and Implementation of Acoustics boces with golden proportions

The following paper presents the design and process of construction of a subwoofer which incorporates all the theory related to the Fibonacci spiral in order to minimize the influence of the normal modes of vibration in chambers, making then the frequency response more accessible to prediction. This work presents the acoustic box design using the theory related to band pass boxes studying each one of the phenomena involved.
